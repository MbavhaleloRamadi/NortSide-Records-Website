About Us

NortSide Records is a music publishing and distribution company based in South Africa. Founded by Mbavhalelo Innocent Ramadi, we work with talented artists and small businesses to bring high-quality music to the world.

Our Mission

At NortSide Records, our mission is to help artists grow and succeed by offering a wide range of professional music services. From distribution to mixing, mastering, and music consulting, we are committed to providing support every step of the way. We believe in the power of music to change lives, and we aim to help our artists achieve their dreams.

Our Vision

We envision a world where artists can express themselves freely and reach a global audience with their music. As we expand, we are constantly evolving to meet the demands of the ever-changing music industry, keeping our focus on the success of our artists and partners.

Meet Our Founder

Mbavhalelo Innocent Ramadi, the visionary behind NortSide Records, founded the company in 2022 with a passion for supporting emerging artists. With years of experience in the music industry, he is dedicated to bringing fresh talent to the forefront and helping them thrive.
